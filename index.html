Frequently Asked Questions

Any questions? We've answered the most common ones below!

What is the ECN Trading Commission Cashback?

This promotion refunds your ECN trading commissions as real cashback. For every 1 standard lot of Forex or Gold traded during the campaign period, you receive $5 USD in real cash — credited directly to your trading account after the campaign ends. The maximum cashback is capped at 50 lots ($250 USD).

Who is eligible for this promotion?

This promotion is open to new TMGM clients only — clients who have not made any deposits before with TMGM on a client level. You must have an ECN account and be registered in one of the 17 eligible countries. Participants must opt-in via their Account Manager.

Is the cashback real cash or bonus credit?

It's real cash — not bonus credit. The cashback is credited directly to your trading account after the campaign ends. Unlike bonus credit offers from other brokers, this is withdrawable cash with no complex conditions attached.

Which products are eligible?

Only Forex and Gold (XAUUSD) products are eligible for this cashback promotion. Trading volume on other products will not be counted towards the cashback.

Are there any trading rules I should be aware of?

Yes. Only trades that are both opened and closed within the promotion period (1–30 April 2026) will count towards your cashback. Positions opened before the promotion period but closed during it will not be counted. Additionally, all trades must be held for at least 2 minutes to qualify.

When will I receive my cashback?

Your cashback will be calculated and reviewed after the campaign ends (30 April 2026). Once approved, the cashback will typically be credited to your trading account within a few business days.

Which countries are eligible for this promotion?

This promotion is open to TMGM clients registered in: Germany, Netherlands, Italy, Belgium, Poland, Croatia, Czech Republic, Turkey, Greece, Nigeria, Kenya, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Jordan, Bahrain, Kuwait, and Oman.
